1. Zlatko Minev

Zlatko Minev is the team and tech lead of the IBM Quantum’s Qiskit Leap research and device design & analysis Qiskit Metal teams.

Talk Description: What will I get if I run my quantum computation on a real quantum computer? Why won’t I get what I expect? In this lecture, we will introduce the basic ideas you need to understand real quantum devices and calculations. We will learn about quantum noise and how to begin to deal with it in modern quantum processors.
